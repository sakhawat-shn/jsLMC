3 digit(Yxx) numeric instruction where 1st digit(Y) is command,2nd and 3rd digits(xx) is memory cell number....
In 1st digit....

		1 means ADD the number(xx) to ALU and store in ALU.
		2 means SUB the number(xx) from ALU and store in ALU.
		3 means STORE ALU in (xx).
		4 means nothing(not implemented.)
		5 means LOAD (xx) in ALU.
		6 means BRANCH unconditional.
		7 means BRANCH if zero.(if ALU 000)
		8 means BRANCH if positive.(if ALU positive)
		9 means {901 for INPUT...
				 902 for OUTPUT...}(to and from ALU.)
		0 means HALT
